4. Email Data and Privacy

DraftMate accesses the content of emails you open (subject line, sender information, and body text) solely to generate a reply draft. This data is transmitted to a secure backend server and then to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. No email content is stored permanently. Data is processed in real-time and discarded immediately after the draft is generated.

By using DraftMate, you consent to this processing of your email content for the sole purpose of generating reply drafts. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for full details.

5. Google and Microsoft Accounts

DraftMate integrates with Gmail via Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol. Your Google account credentials are never seen or stored by DraftMate. Authentication is handled entirely by Google's identity platform through the Chrome identity API. Your use of Gmail is also subject to Google's Terms of Service.

For Outlook Web, DraftMate does not store any Microsoft account credentials. Your use of Outlook is subject to Microsoft's Service Agreement.
