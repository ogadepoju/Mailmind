The story
"I'm just waiting for AI to reply my emails — I literally say the same thing every time."

My professor Dr. Jess said that in class one day, half joking. I wasn't joking at all.

I went home and started building DraftMate — a Chrome extension that drafts email replies for you the moment you open a message. No blank screens. No copy-pasting between tabs. Just open, review, and send.

After months of building, debugging DOM structures across two email clients, and a couple of Chrome Web Store rejections — it's live. Free. For anyone who spends too much time in their inbox.

— Odunayo Gabriel, University of Cincinnati
